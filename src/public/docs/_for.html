for

clojure.core/for

([seq-exprs body-expr])

Macro List comprehension. Takes a vector of one or more binding-form/collection-expr pairs, each followed by zero or more modifiers, and yields a lazy sequence of evaluations of expr. Collections are iterated in a nested fashion, rightmost fastest, and nested coll-exprs can refer to bindings created in prior binding-forms. Supported modifiers are: :let [binding-form expr ...], :while test, :when test.

(take 100 (for [x (range 100000000) y (range 1000000) :while (< y x)] [x y]))
